About me

I am a Carnegie Mellon University graduate of Master of Science in Robotics (MSR) under Prof. Sebastian Scherer in the AirLab.

Prior to that, I completed my B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering with an additional major in Robotics at CMU, working with Prof. Howie Choset in the Biorobotics Lab.

Currently I am doing part-time research for Prof. Peng Yin of City University of Hong Kong and AI4CE Lab at New York University.

My current research interests lie in Localization, Mapping, Vision Place Recognition, Multi-Robot Systems, Mobile Platforms.
